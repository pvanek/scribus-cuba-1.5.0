Contributions to the documentation

This online help is the fourth generation of the Scribus documentation. The initial documents, from which these were developed were written by Peter Linnell with help from Franz Schmid. Since then, the Scribus Team has grown, as did the number of contributions from other people, and we have seen an increasing number of additions and updates. The following table attempts to list the contributors to the documentations. Hopefully we didn't miss anyone out (if so, please contact us and we will add you in):
